Waiwai Soup Recipe

Ingredients

The ingredients required to make a pot of Waiwai are:

  • 200ml of water
  • A packet of Waiwai Noodle
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

You should follow these instructions to make a hot soup of Waiwai.

  1. Take 200ml of water in a pan.
  2. Boil water for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the packet of Waiwai in the boiling water.
  4. Add the seasoning powder from the packet.
  5. Add some salt according to your taste.
  6. Cover the pan with a plate.
  7. Let Waiwai boil for 3-5 minutes.
  8. After 5 minutes, serve it in a bowl.

You can enjoy your hot Waiwai noodle soup watching TMKOC.
